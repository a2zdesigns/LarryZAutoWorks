Larry'Z AutoWorks is a family-owned and operated business that was established in 1978 by Larry Zimmerman. With a love for and tinkering with automobiles, he set out to make a name for himself. He strived to provide quality repair with an honest outlook. Now with 40+ years in business, Larry'Z has become a well known name in the Sacramento area. In early 2000, Larry took his son, Aaron, under his wing and today Aaron continues to run the business. He carries on the tradition of a reliable and honest auto shop. Larry'Z namesake passed away in 2019, but his name and legend lives on in the heart of Larry'Z AutoWorks.

  • We take pride in offering honest repair advice about the condition of your vehicles and repair options based on need, not greed.
  • We are dedicated to keeping open lines of communication with our customers and exceed your expectations.
  • We have an unmatched reputation within our community and are known for our reliable automotive service with superior quality.
